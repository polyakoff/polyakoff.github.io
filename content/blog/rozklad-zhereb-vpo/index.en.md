The «Housing for IDPs» programme (a 3% subsidised mortgage from Ukraine's State Youth Housing Fund) allocates slots by an open draw. There is no fixed calendar — each stage is announced separately, whenever the next tranche from international partners arrives. Below is the current status, refreshed automatically from the Fund's site.

## How it works

The selection is a random computer pick (the RandomPicker.com service), run live on the Fund's YouTube channel. Dates are announced roughly two weeks ahead. Winners are published during the broadcast and on the Fund's national and regional sites.

## Primary sources

- [Fund news](https://www.molod-kredit.gov.ua/pres-tsentr/novyny) — announcements and results for each stage.
- [IDP housing loan (Diia)](https://diia.gov.ua/services/kredit-na-zhitlo-dlya-vpo) — apply and check status.
